CyberCate

Overview: This website will teach you about the cybersecurity basics using modules with quizzes at the end.

Instructions: Begin by clicking on the module to access the instructional video. After watching the video, carefully review the information provided on the page. Once you feel confident in your understanding, proceed to complete the accompanying quiz. It is imperative that each quiz within a module attains a perfect score of 100% in order to unlock the subsequent module. Upon successful completion of all modules, the final assessment will be unlocked, taking knowledge from each individual module into a comprehensive exam.
